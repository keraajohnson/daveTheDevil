A "Hot" new graphic novel written and illustated by (Viking) Peter Smits

Dave The Devil tells a story of growth and change as the prince of darkness himself become overly bored with everything wicked. His fascination with humankind leads him on a journey to “the surface”, filled with hope and regret and determination. From an unenthusiastic devil to the top cop in the city, Dave learns valuable lessons, finds purpose and becomes a hero to the humans. Then of course he loses everything and tries to get it all back, but not without letting go of something he holds dear.
